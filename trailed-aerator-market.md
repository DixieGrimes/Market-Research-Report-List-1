<p><p>The Trailed Aerator Market is categorized into Vertical Axis and Horizontal Axis types, each designed for specific aeration needs in agricultural and turf management. Vertical Axis Aerators utilize vertically oriented blades to penetrate soil deeply, enhancing air and water infiltration. In contrast, Horizontal Axis Aerators employ a horizontal design to cover larger areas efficiently, delivering uniform aeration.
