<p><p>The long-range camera market is divided into two main types: long-range thermal cameras and non-thermal long-range cameras. Long-range thermal cameras detect infrared radiation, enabling visibility in complete darkness and through obstructions like smoke or fog, making them ideal for surveillance and search-and-rescue operations. In contrast, non-thermal long-range cameras capture high-resolution images and videos using visible light, often equipped with advanced optics for enhanced clarity, making them suitable for applications such as wildlife observation and perimeter security.</p></p>
