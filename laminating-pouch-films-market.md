<p><p>Laminating pouch films are essential for protecting documents and enhancing durability through lamination. Heat-sealed films require a heat source to bond the film to the material, providing a strong, long-lasting seal ideal for thicker or larger items. Conversely, cold pouch films use adhesive properties to seal without heat, making them convenient for sensitive materials that may be damaged by high temperatures. Both types cater to different applications, ensuring effective protection and presentation for various documents and items.</p></p>
