<p><p>Non-thermal pasteurization equipment includes several technologies designed to extend shelf life while preserving quality. High Pressure Processing (HPP) uses high pressure to eliminate pathogens without heat. Pulse Electric Field (PEF) applies short bursts of electricity to disrupt microbial cell membranes. Microwave Volumetric Heating (MVH) heats food uniformly using microwaves. Irradiation involves exposing food to ionizing radiation to kill microorganisms. Ultrasonic technology employs high-frequency sound waves for food treatment. Other methods may include innovative approaches not listed, all focused on safety and quality preservation.</p></p>
