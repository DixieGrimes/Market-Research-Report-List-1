<p><p>The Titanium Diboride market can be segmented based on production methods: the Carbothermal reduction method and the Self-propagating High-temperature Synthesis (SHS) method. The Carbothermal reduction method involves heating titanium dioxide and boron carbide with carbon, resulting in titanium diboride. In contrast, the SHS method uses an exothermic reaction between titanium and boron powder, producing titanium diboride while generating its own heat. Both methods cater to different industrial applications, influencing market dynamics and product availability in sectors like aerospace and ceramics.</p></p>
