<p><p>In the medical imaging market, X-ray detectors are categorized into dynamic and static imaging types. Dynamic imaging detectors capture moving images in real-time, enabling clinicians to observe physiological functions and assess conditions like blood flow or organ motion. In contrast, static imaging detectors produce still images for diagnostic purposes, focusing on structural details and anomalies within the body. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient care in various medical scenarios.</p></p>
