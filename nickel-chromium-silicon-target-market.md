<p><p>The Nickel Chromium Silicon target market comprises two primary types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary substrates used in various deposition processes, offering uniform coating capabilities. They are typically favored in applications requiring consistent film thickness. Rotating targets, on the other hand, involve cylindrical components that rotate during deposition, enhancing uniformity and extending target life by distributing wear evenly. Both types serve crucial roles in industries like electronics and solar cell manufacturing, facilitating advanced material applications.</p></p>
