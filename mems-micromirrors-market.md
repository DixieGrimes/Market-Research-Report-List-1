<p><p>The MEMS micromirrors market is categorized into mono-axial and dual-axial mirrors. Mono-axial MEMS mirrors can tilt around a single axis, making them suitable for applications like optical switches and beam steering. In contrast, dual-axial MEMS mirrors can tilt around two axes, enabling more complex movements for applications such as advanced imaging systems and 3D scanning. The distinction between these types allows for targeted solutions across industries like telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
