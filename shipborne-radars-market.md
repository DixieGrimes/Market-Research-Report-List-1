<p><ul><li>Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) Radar</li><li>Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) Radar</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shipborne radars market includes various types, primarily Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) and Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) radars. PESA radars use a single transmitter and multiple receivers, enabling rapid beam steering and improved target detection. In contrast, AESA radars feature individual transmit/receive modules for each antenna element, allowing for greater flexibility, enhanced performance, and the capacity to track multiple targets simultaneously. Other radar types may include traditional mechanical systems and emerging technologies, catering to diverse naval requirements.</p></p>
