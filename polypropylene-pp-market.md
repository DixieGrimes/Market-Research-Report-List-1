<p><strong>The Polypropylene (PP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymer</li><li>Copolymer</li><li>Impact copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) market is categorized into three main types: homopolymer, copolymer, and impact copolymer. Homopolymer PP is characterized by its rigidity and high tensile strength, making it ideal for rigid packaging and automotive components. Copolymer PP, which includes random and block variants, offers improved flexibility and transparency, suitable for applications like containers and consumer goods. Impact copolymer PP combines enhanced toughness and resistance to environmental stress, making it perfect for demanding applications such as automotive parts and industrial applications.</p></p>
