<p><p>Monolithic HPLC columns are categorized into silica-based and polymer-based types. Silica-based columns utilize porous silica structures, allowing for efficient separation and higher resolution in liquid chromatography applications. They are widely favored for their compatibility with a range of mobile phases. Conversely, polymer-based columns use cross-linked polymer materials, offering increased chemical stability and resistance to aggressive solvents. This makes them ideal for specific applications where silica may not perform well, enhancing flexibility in method development and analysis across various industries.</p></p>
