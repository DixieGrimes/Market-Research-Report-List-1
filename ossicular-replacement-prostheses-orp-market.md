<p><p>Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (ORP) are medical devices used to reconstruct the ossicular chain in the middle ear, often following damage or disease. The market includes Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (PORP) that replace one or more, but not all, of the ossicles, allowing for some natural structure to remain. Total Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (TORP) replace the entire ossicular chain, providing complete reconstruction. Both types are essential in restoring hearing function and are used in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
