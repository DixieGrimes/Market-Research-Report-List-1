<p><p>The PVDF coated battery separator market is categorized into two main types: water-based and oil-based. Water-based separators utilize aqueous solutions for coating, offering advantages such as reduced environmental impact and improved safety. They enhance ion conductivity while providing mechanical strength. In contrast, oil-based separators rely on organic solvents for coating, providing superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse battery technologies, including lithium-ion and lead-acid batteries, addressing specific performance needs.</p></p>
