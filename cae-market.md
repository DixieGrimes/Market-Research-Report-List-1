Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulates fluid flow and heat transfer, crucial for optimizing designs in engineering. Multibody Dynamics analyzes the interaction of interconnected bodies in motion, aiding mechanical design. Finally, Optimization and Simulation techniques enhance design efficiency, exploring various parameters to achieve optimal performance in products and systems across diverse industries.</p></p>
