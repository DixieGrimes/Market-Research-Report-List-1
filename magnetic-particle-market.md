<p><p>The magnetic particle market is primarily categorized into two types: ferrites and NdFeB (Neodymium-Iron-Boron). Ferrites are ceramic compounds known for their lower cost and good magnetic properties, commonly used in applications like transformers and inductors. NdFeB, on the other hand, represents a more advanced permanent magnet technology, offering higher magnetic strength and efficiency, making it ideal for high-performance applications such as electric motors and computer hard drives. Both types serve distinct market needs based on their properties and application demands.</p></p>
