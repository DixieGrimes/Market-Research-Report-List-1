<p><p>The nonfat dry milk powder market is categorized into three main types based on processing heat levels: low heat, medium heat, and high heat. Low heat nonfat dry milk is produced at lower temperatures, retaining more of its nutritional quality and is ideal for reconstitution in baked goods and creamers. Medium heat varieties strike a balance between stability and flavor, suitable for various food applications. High heat nonfat dry milk, processed at higher temperatures, offers improved shelf life and is often used in dairy products and infant formulas.</p></p>
