<p><ul><li>Non-contact Liquid Handling Workstation</li><li>Contact Type Liquid Handling Workstation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fully Automated Liquid Handling Workstation market is categorized into two primary types: non-contact and contact liquid handling workstations. Non-contact liquid handling workstations utilize methods such as air displacement or acoustic droplet ejection to dispense liquids without physical contact, minimizing contamination risk. In contrast, contact-type liquid handling workstations involve physical interaction with liquids through pipetting or other mechanisms, ensuring precise volume transfer but potentially increasing contamination risk. Both types cater to diverse applications in laboratories, pharmaceuticals, and research settings.</p></p>
