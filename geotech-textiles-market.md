<p><p>The geotech textiles market comprises several types, including woven, non-woven, knitted fabrics, and others. Woven fabrics are constructed by interlacing yarns, offering high tensile strength and durability, ideal for erosion control and reinforcement. Non-woven fabrics, created through bonding fibers, provide excellent filtration and drainage properties, making them suitable for geotextile applications like separators and liners. Knitted fabrics, characterized by their flexibility and stretch, are used in specialized applications. The "others" category includes composites and specialized materials tailored for specific geotechnical needs.</p></p>
