<p><ul><li>Diode Type</li><li>Relay Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery isolator market primarily includes two types: diode isolators and relay isolators. Diode types utilize semiconductor diodes to prevent backflow of current, offering a compact, reliable solution with minimal voltage drop. Relay types, on the other hand, use mechanical relays to disconnect batteries, providing a more robust approach with higher current handling capabilities. Both types serve to protect batteries in automotive and marine applications, ensuring efficient power distribution and enhancing battery life by preventing cross-drainage.</p></p>
