<p><ul><li>Oscillatory Motion</li><li>Rotary Motion</li><li>Linear Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical actuator market is categorized into three main types based on motion: oscillatory, rotary, and linear. Oscillatory motion actuators produce back-and-forth movement, commonly used in applications like valves and mixers. Rotary motion actuators generate rotational movement, ideal for tasks such as robotic joints and conveyor systems. Linear motion actuators create straight-line movement, frequently applied in automation, material handling, and precision positioning. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driving innovation and efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
