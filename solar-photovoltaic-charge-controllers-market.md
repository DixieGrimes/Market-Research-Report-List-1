<p><ul><li>PWM Type</li><li>MPPT Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar Photovoltaic Charge Controllers market comprises two primary types: PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) and MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking). PWM controllers are simpler and more cost-effective, regulating voltage and current by varying the pulse width to charge batteries efficiently at lower capacities. In contrast, MPPT controllers optimize energy harvest from solar panels by adjusting parameters to operate at peak efficiency, making them suitable for larger systems. Both types play a crucial role in managing solar energy storage effectively.</p></p>
