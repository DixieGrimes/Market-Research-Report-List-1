<p><p>The Vehicle GPS Locator market consists of three main types: Cellular, Wireless Passive, and Satellite Real-Time systems. Cellular GPS locators utilize mobile networks for real-time tracking and data transmission, offering broad coverage. Wireless Passive systems rely on passive signals and usually require proximity to a reader for tracking, making them simpler but less versatile. Satellite Real-Time systems provide global coverage and continuous tracking via satellite communication, making them ideal for remote areas but often at a higher cost.</p></p>
