<p><strong>The Medical Packaging Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical packaging solutions market is divided into two main types: primary and secondary packaging. Primary packaging involves the immediate packaging that directly contains the medical product, such as blister packs, vials, and syringes, ensuring the product's safety and integrity. Secondary packaging refers to the outer packaging that groups primary packages together, such as boxes and cartons, often used for storage and transportation. Both types play crucial roles in compliance, protection, and effective distribution of medical products.</p></p>
