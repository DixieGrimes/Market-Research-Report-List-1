<p><p>The fire suppression products market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Dry Chemical Fire Suppression** utilizes a fine powder that interrupts the chemical reaction in a fire, effective for various fire classes.</p><p>2. **Foam Fire Suppression** creates a blanket over flammable liquids, smothering flames and preventing re-ignition.</p><p>3. **CO2 Fire Suppression** employs carbon dioxide to displace oxygen, effectively suffocating fires, particularly in enclosed spaces.</p><p>4. **Others** include water-based systems, wet chemical suppressants, and specialized agents for unique applications, catering to diverse fire protection needs.</p></p>
