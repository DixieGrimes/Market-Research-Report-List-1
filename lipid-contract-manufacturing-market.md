<p><p>The lipid contract manufacturing market encompasses the production of lipid-based delivery systems, primarily focusing on liposomes and lipid nanoparticles. Liposomes are spherical vesicles composed of phospholipid bilayers, used for encapsulating drugs and improving bioavailability. Lipid nanoparticles, on the other hand, are solid lipid carriers that enhance the stability and release of therapeutic agents. Both types are essential in pharmaceutical formulations, offering targeted delivery and reduced side effects, thus driving their demand in drug development and biopharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
