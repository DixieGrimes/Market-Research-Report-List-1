<p><p>The Precision Seamless Pipe market is primarily categorized into two types: Cold Drawn and Cold Rolled pipes. Cold Drawn pipes undergo a process where they are pulled through a die to achieve precise dimensions and a smooth finish, enhancing strength and durability. In contrast, Cold Rolled pipes are produced by rolling at room temperature, allowing for better mechanical properties and tighter tolerances. Both types find applications in industries requiring high precision and reliability, such as automotive and construction.</p></p>
