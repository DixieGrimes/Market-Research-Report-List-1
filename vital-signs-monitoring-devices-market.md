<p><p>The vital signs monitoring devices market comprises traditional monitoring devices and microwave monitoring devices. Traditional monitoring devices include technologies like electrocardiograms and pulse oximeters, which have been widely used for years to assess heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and other vital signs. Conversely, microwave monitoring devices leverage advanced microwave radar technology to non-invasively track vital signs, offering real-time data and improved patient comfort. This innovative approach addresses limitations of traditional devices by enabling continuous monitoring with minimal patient interaction.</p></p>
