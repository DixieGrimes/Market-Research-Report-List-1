<p><strong>The Organic Light-emitting Diodes (OLEDs) Microdisplay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Reflective Microdisplay"</li><li>"Transmissive Microdisplay"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED microdisplay market is primarily categorized into reflective and transmissive microdisplays. Reflective microdisplays utilize ambient light to enhance visibility, making them ideal for applications like augmented reality and wearable devices. They reflect light from a backlight source rather than emitting it, providing high brightness and contrast. In contrast, transmissive microdisplays emit light directly, offering superior color accuracy and resolution, making them suitable for devices such as virtual reality headsets and high-definition displays. Both types cater to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
