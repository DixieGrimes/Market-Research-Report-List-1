<p><p>The Display Fingerprint Technology market encompasses various identification methods, notably Optical Identification and Ultrasound Identification. Optical Identification uses light to capture detailed fingerprint images, leveraging high-resolution sensors for fast and accurate recognition. In contrast, Ultrasound Identification employs sound waves to penetrate the skin and capture unique fingerprint details, providing enhanced security and performance in diverse conditions. Both technologies are crucial for biometric authentication in smartphones, tablets, and other electronic devices, catering to the growing demand for secure access solutions.</p></p>
