<p><p>Transformers for frequency converters are essential in converting electrical power between different frequency levels. The market is divided into two main types: dry type and oil-immersed transformers. </p><p>Dry type transformers utilize air for cooling and are preferred for indoor applications due to their low maintenance and reduced environmental risks. Conversely, oil-immersed transformers use oil for cooling and insulation, enabling higher power handling, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing energy efficiency and performance in frequency conversion processes.</p></p>
