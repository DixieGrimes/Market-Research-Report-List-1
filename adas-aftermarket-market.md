<p><p>The ADAS aftermarket encompasses various sensor technologies crucial for vehicle safety and automation. Ultrasonic sensors detect nearby objects using sound waves, ideal for parking assistance. Lidar sensors use laser pulses for precise 3D mapping, enhancing autonomous navigation. Radar sensors rely on radio waves for long-range object detection, essential for adaptive cruise control. Camera sensors provide visual data for lane departure and traffic sign recognition. Infrared sensors detect heat signatures for night vision. Pressure sensors monitor tire pressure, ensuring stability and safety. Each technology plays a vital role in advancing ADAS capabilities.</p></p>
