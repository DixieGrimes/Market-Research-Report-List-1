<p><p>The stationary battery testing equipment market is categorized into three main types: cell testing, module testing, and pack testing. Cell testing evaluates individual battery cells for performance and safety, ensuring each component meets required standards. Module testing focuses on assessing groups of cells assembled into a module, examining their collective efficiency and reliability. Pack testing evaluates the entire battery pack, which includes multiple modules, to determine overall performance, safety, and compatibility within larger energy systems or applications.</p></p>
