<p><p>The oil diffusion vacuum pump market is primarily segmented into two types: single-stage pumps and multistage pumps. Single-stage pumps operate effectively for achieving low to medium vacuum levels, utilizing a single rotating element for gas movement. In contrast, multistage pumps consist of multiple pumping stages, allowing for higher vacuum levels and improved efficiency in gas compression. These pumps are essential in various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing and scientific research, where precise vacuum conditions are critical for optimal performance.</p></p>
