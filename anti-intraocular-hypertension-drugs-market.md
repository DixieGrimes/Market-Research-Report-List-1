<p><strong>The Anti-intraocular Hypertension Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Agonist</li><li>Beta Blockers</li><li>Prostaglandin Analogs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-intraocular hypertension drugs market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Alpha Agonists**: These reduce aqueous humor production and increase outflow, effectively lowering intraocular pressure.</p><p>2. **Beta Blockers**: They decrease aqueous humor secretion, providing another method for pressure control.</p><p>3. **Prostaglandin Analogs**: These enhance uveoscleral outflow, significantly lowering intraocular pressure with once-daily dosing.</p><p>4. **Others**: This category includes carbonic anhydrase inhibitors and Rho kinase inhibitors, which use different mechanisms to achieve similar pressure-lowering effects.</p></p>
