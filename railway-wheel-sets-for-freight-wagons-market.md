<p><p>Railway wheel sets for freight wagons comprise primarily forged and cast wheels, each offering distinct advantages. Forged wheels are created through a process that involves shaping heated metal, resulting in superior strength, durability, and resistance to wear, making them ideal for heavy loads. Conversely, casting wheels are produced by pouring molten metal into molds, allowing for complex designs and cost-effective production, though they may have lower strength compared to forged options. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient freight transport.</p></p>
