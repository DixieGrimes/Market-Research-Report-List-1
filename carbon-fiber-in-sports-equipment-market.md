<p><p>Carbon fiber in sports equipment is categorized primarily into three types: PAN-based, pitch-based, and others. PAN-based carbon fiber, derived from polyacrylonitrile, is widely used due to its high strength-to-weight ratio and excellent mechanical properties, making it ideal for high-performance sports gear. Pitch-based carbon fiber, while less common, offers superior thermal and electrical properties, suitable for specific applications. The "others" category includes alternative production methods and hybrid fibers that combine carbon with other materials to enhance performance and durability in diverse sports equipment.</p></p>
