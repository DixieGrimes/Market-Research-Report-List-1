<p><p>Tangential Flow Filtration (TFF) is a separation technique widely used in biopharmaceutical and biotechnology industries for purifying and concentrating biomolecules, such as proteins, nucleic acids, and viruses. Unlike traditional filtration methods, TFF circulates the feed stream along the filter membrane's surface, allowing for effective separation while minimizing fouling. This technique is particularly valued for its high efficiency and scalability, making it instrumental in various applications, including vaccine production, monoclonal antibody manufacturing, and cell culture.</p><p>The Tangential Flow Filtration Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for biopharmaceuticals and advancements in filtration technologies.
