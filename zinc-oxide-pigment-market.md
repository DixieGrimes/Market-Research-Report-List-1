<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Pigment market comprises two primary types: powder and dust. Powdered zinc oxide is finely milled for applications in coatings, plastics, and cosmetics, offering high opacity and UV protection. It’s widely used in industrial and artistic products. Conversely, dust zinc oxide refers to a coarser texture, suitable for applications in agriculture and rubber manufacturing, where it serves as a filler and reinforcing agent. Both forms leverage zinc oxide's beneficial properties, including durability and non-toxicity.</p></p>
