<p><ul><li>Non-clumping</li><li>Clumping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly cat litter market comprises two primary types: non-clumping and clumping. Non-clumping litter absorbs moisture without forming solid masses, making it easy to sift through and replace as needed. Common materials include recycled paper, wood pellets, and corn. Conversely, clumping litter forms solid clumps when wet, allowing for efficient removal of waste, leading to less frequent full changes. This type often utilizes natural materials like bentonite clay or plant-based products, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking convenience alongside sustainability.</p></p>
