<p><p>The Voice Coil Motor (VCM) industry primarily features two market types: AF VCM (Autofocus Voice Coil Motor) and OIS VCM (Optical Image Stabilization Voice Coil Motor). AF VCMs are predominantly utilized in cameras and smartphones to achieve precise focusing, enhancing image quality. In contrast, OIS VCMs help stabilize images by compensating for camera shakes during capture, ideal for both photography and videography. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in improving optical performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
