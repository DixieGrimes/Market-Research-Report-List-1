<p><ul><li>Track Chains</li><li>Track Roller or Carrier Roller</li><li>Track Shoes</li><li>Sprockets and Segments</li><li>Idlers and Tension Groups</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The undercarriage components market encompasses various essential parts for heavy machinery. Track chains facilitate movement by connecting the track shoes, which provide traction on surfaces. Track rollers, also known as carrier rollers, support the weight of the vehicle and assist in reducing friction. Sprockets and segments ensure efficient power transfer from the engine to the tracks. Idlers help guide the track and maintain tension, which is critical for optimal performance and longevity of the equipment.</p></p>
