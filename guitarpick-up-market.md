<p><strong>The GuitarPick Up Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Hole Pickup</li><li>Piezoelectric Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar pickup market includes various types, primarily sound hole pickups and piezoelectric pickups. Sound hole pickups are typically used in acoustic guitars and are designed to capture vibrations from the strings through the sound hole, emphasizing warm tones. In contrast, piezoelectric pickups are installed under the bridge and convert mechanical stress from the strings and body into electrical signals, offering a clearer sound and greater dynamic range. Both types cater to different playing styles and tonal preferences.</p></p>
