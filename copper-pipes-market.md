<p><p>The copper pipes market consists of three main types: Type K, Type L, and Type M. Type K pipes have the thickest walls and are ideal for underground and high-pressure applications, offering superior durability. Type L pipes are slightly thinner, making them versatile for various plumbing and heating systems. Type M pipes have the thinnest walls, designed primarily for light-duty applications like residential plumbing. Each type serves distinct purposes, impacting their demand, pricing, and applications in construction and industrial sectors.</p></p>
