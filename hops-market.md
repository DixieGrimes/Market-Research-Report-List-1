<p><strong>The Hops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bittering Hops</li><li>Aroma Hops</li><li>Dual-Purpose Hops</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hops are categorized into three main market types based on their primary uses in brewing. Bittering hops are primarily used for their alpha acids, providing bitterness to balance the sweetness of malt. Aroma hops, on the other hand, are prized for their fragrant oils, contributing desirable flavors and aromas to the beer without imparting significant bitterness. Dual-purpose hops serve both functions, offering bitterness and aromatic qualities, making them versatile for brewers looking to simplify their hop additions.</p></p>
