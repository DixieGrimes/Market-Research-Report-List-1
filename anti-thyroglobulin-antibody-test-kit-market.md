<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Time-resolved Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-thyroglobulin Antibody Test Kit market is segmented into two primary types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). CLIA utilizes light-emitting reactions to detect antibody levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results, making it popular in clinical settings. Conversely, TRIA employs time-resolved fluorescence to measure antibodies, ensuring high specificity and reduced background noise. Both methods are essential for diagnosing autoimmune thyroid diseases, providing healthcare professionals with critical insights for effective patient management.</p></p>
