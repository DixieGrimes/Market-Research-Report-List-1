<p><ul><li>Load Bearing</li><li>Non-loaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car chassis frame market is primarily divided into two types: load-bearing and non-loaded frames. Load-bearing frames are designed to support the vehicle's weight and withstand various stresses during operation, making them integral to the car's structural integrity. In contrast, non-loaded frames do not bear significant weight; instead, they mainly serve as a support structure for other components without contributing to the vehicle's overall strength. Each type is chosen based on the vehicle's design, purpose, and performance requirements.</p></p>
