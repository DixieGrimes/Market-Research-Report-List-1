<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Oxygen Regulator</li><li>Multi-Stage Oxygen Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical oxygen regulators market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage oxygen regulators. Single-stage regulators provide a single reduction in oxygen pressure, offering simplicity and ease of use, often suitable for portable applications. In contrast, multi-stage regulators feature multiple pressure reduction stages, allowing for more precise control of oxygen delivery and enhanced stability, making them ideal for stationary use in hospitals or critical care settings. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safe and effective oxygen therapy.</p></p>
