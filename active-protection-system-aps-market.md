<p><strong>The Active Protection System (APS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard-kill</li><li>Soft-kill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Protection System (APS) market is categorized into Hard-kill and Soft-kill systems. Hard-kill APS physically intercepts and destroys incoming threats like missiles or rockets using countermeasures such as projectiles or explosives. Soft-kill APS employs non-lethal methods, such as jamming, decoys, or laser dazzlers, to disrupt or mislead enemy targeting systems, rendering threats ineffective. Both systems enhance vehicle and personnel survivability in combat, with Hard-kill focusing on direct neutralization and Soft-kill on deception and disruption of enemy systems.</p></p>
