<p><p>The magnesium carbonate minerals market primarily consists of two key types: magnesite and hydromagnesite. Magnesite, a naturally occurring mineral, is primarily used in refractories, agriculture, and environmental applications due to its high magnesium content. Hydromagnesite, a hydrated form of magnesium carbonate, is valued for its applications in flame retardants, chemical processes, and as a filler in various industries. Both minerals are crucial in sectors like construction, automotive, and environmental management, driving demand across diverse markets.</p></p>
