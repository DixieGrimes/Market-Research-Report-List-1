<p><strong>The Fifth Wheel Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compensating</li><li>Semi-oscillating</li><li>Fully Oscillating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fifth wheel couplings market features three primary types: compensating, semi-oscillating, and fully oscillating couplings. Compensating couplings allow for vertical and lateral movement, accommodating slight misalignments. Semi-oscillating couplings permit more freedom of movement compared to compensating types, allowing for enhanced stability while still managing some misalignment. Fully oscillating couplings provide maximum mobility, allowing for extensive movement in all directions, which is ideal for rough terrain and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific demands in transport and logistics.</p></p>
