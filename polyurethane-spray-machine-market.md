<p><p>The polyurethane spray machine market comprises three main types: air-powered, electric, and hydraulic. Air-powered machines utilize compressed air to atomize polyurethane for application, offering portability and ease of use. Electric machines provide consistent power, leading to more precise control and reduced emissions, making them ideal for indoor applications. Hydraulic machines rely on hydraulic pressure to deliver high output and are suited for large-scale projects requiring high viscosity materials. Each type caters to varying needs in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
