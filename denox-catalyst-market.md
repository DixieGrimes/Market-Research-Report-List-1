<p><p>The DeNOx catalyst market is primarily segmented into two types: honeycomb and flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts feature a porous, cylindrical structure that enhances surface area for efficient nitrogen oxide conversion, commonly used in automotive applications. Flat catalysts, on the other hand, have a simpler, flat design, which is often utilized in industrial processes where space is limited. Both types aim to reduce harmful emissions, but their designs cater to different applications and performance requirements in exhaust gas cleaning systems.</p></p>
