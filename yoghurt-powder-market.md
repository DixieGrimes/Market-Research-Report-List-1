<p><p>The yogurt powder market consists of three primary types: skim yogurt powder, low fat yogurt powder, and full cream milk yogurt powder. Skim yogurt powder is made from non-fat yogurt, offering a lower calorie option. Low fat yogurt powder contains some fat for a creamier texture while maintaining reduced calories, making it a balanced choice. Full cream milk yogurt powder retains the original fat content, delivering a rich flavor and creaminess, appealing to those seeking indulgence in their yogurt products.</p></p>
