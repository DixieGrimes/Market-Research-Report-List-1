<p><ul><li>Contact Cleaning Robot</li><li>Non-contact Cleaning Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Building Facade Cleaning Robot market is divided into two main types: contact cleaning robots and non-contact cleaning robots. </p><p>Contact cleaning robots utilize physical brushes, pads, or specialized tools to scrub surfaces, effectively removing dirt and residues. These are often used for intensive cleaning tasks on varied facade materials. </p><p>In contrast, non-contact cleaning robots employ methods like water jets, air pressure, or ultrasonic waves to clean surfaces without direct contact, minimizing the risk of damage to delicate structures, while still maintaining cleanliness and efficiency.</p></p>
