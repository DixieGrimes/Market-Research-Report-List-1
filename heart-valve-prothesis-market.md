<p><p>The heart valve prosthesis market includes three main types: Transcatheter Heart Valves, Tissue Heart Valves, and Mechanical Heart Valves. Transcatheter Heart Valves are designed for minimally invasive procedures, reducing recovery time. Tissue Heart Valves, made from biological materials, offer a natural feel and may improve patient acceptance but can degenerate over time. Mechanical Heart Valves, crafted from durable materials, provide longevity but require lifelong anticoagulation therapy. Each type caters to different patient needs and clinical scenarios in cardiac care.</p></p>
