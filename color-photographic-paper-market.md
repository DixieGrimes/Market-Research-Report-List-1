<p><p>The color photographic paper market is primarily divided into two types: silver halide photographic paper and inkjet photographic paper. Silver halide photographic paper employs light-sensitive silver compounds, producing high-quality prints with a traditional photographic process, favored for its rich color and durability. Inkjet photographic paper, on the other hand, is designed for inkjet printers, available in various finishes and textures, offering flexibility and ease of use for digital photography. Each type caters to distinct preferences in image quality and printing technology.</p></p>
