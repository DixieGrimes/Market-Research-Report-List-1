<p><strong>The High Molecular Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Molecular Humidity Sensor market comprises various types, including resistance type, capacitive type, and others. Resistance-type sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical resistance of the sensing material as moisture content varies. Capacitive sensors operate by measuring changes in capacitance resulting from the absorption of water vapor by a dielectric material. Other types may include thermal conductivity and piezoelectric sensors, each utilizing distinct principles to detect humidity levels in various applications, from industrial to consumer products.</p></p>
