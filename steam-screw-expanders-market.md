<p><p>Steam screw expanders are mechanical devices used to convert thermal energy from steam into mechanical work, typically for power generation or industrial processes. They operate on the principle of screw compression in reverse, where high-pressure steam expands within the screw mechanism, driving the rotors and producing rotational energy. These expanders are known for their efficiency, compact design, and ability to handle varying steam conditions, making them suitable for applications in waste heat recovery, geothermal power, and cogeneration systems.</p><p>The Steam Screw Expanders Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
