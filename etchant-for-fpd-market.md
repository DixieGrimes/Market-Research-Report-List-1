Metal etchants are specifically designed to remove metal layers, crucial for precise pattern creation in the manufacturing of displays. Conversely, non-metal etchants target insulating materials and organic films, facilitating processes like layer definition and surface treatment. Both types play essential roles in achieving high-resolution patterns and ensuring the functionality of flat-panel displays in various electronic devices.</p></p>
