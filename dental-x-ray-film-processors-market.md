<p><ul><li>Intraoral Film Processors</li><li>Extraoral Film Processors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental X-ray film processors are essential for developing images used in dental diagnostics. Intraoral film processors handle small film sizes placed inside the mouth, providing detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures. These processors are compact and designed for quick, on-site development. In contrast, extraoral film processors accommodate larger films used outside the mouth, capturing broader images like panoramic views of the dental arch and jaw. Both types are crucial for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning in dentistry.</p></p>
