<p><p>The Satellite IoT market encompasses two primary types: Sat-IoT Backhaul Services and Direct to Satellite Services. Sat-IoT Backhaul Services involve connecting terrestrial IoT networks to satellite systems for data transmission, enabling extended coverage and reliability in remote areas. Conversely, Direct to Satellite Services allow IoT devices to communicate directly with satellites, eliminating the need for ground infrastructure, which is ideal for applications in hard-to-reach locations. Together, these services enhance connectivity for various IoT applications worldwide.</p></p>
