The market is divided into two primary types: polymeric coatings and inorganic hard coatings. Polymeric coatings, typically flexible and easy to apply, provide excellent moisture resistance and insulation. In contrast, inorganic hard coatings are more durable and offer superior protection against abrasion and thermal stress. Both types enhance the reliability and longevity of electronic devices in various applications.</p></p>
