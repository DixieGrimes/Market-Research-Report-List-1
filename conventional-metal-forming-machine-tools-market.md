<p><ul><li>Bulk Deformation (BD)</li><li>Sheet Metal Working (SMW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools market is divided into two main categories: Bulk Deformation (BD) and Sheet Metal Working (SMW). Bulk Deformation involves processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling, focusing on altering the shape of metal in large forms, typically for structural applications. In contrast, Sheet Metal Working encompasses operations such as bending, punching, and stamping, aimed at producing components from thin sheets of metal, often used in consumer products and automotive industries. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs.</p></p>
