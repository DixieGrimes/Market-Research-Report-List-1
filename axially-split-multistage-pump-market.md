<p><p>The axially split multistage pump market can be divided into two main types: single suction pumps and double suction pumps. Single suction pumps draw fluid from one side, typically offering a more straightforward design and lower maintenance needs, making them suitable for lower flow applications. In contrast, double suction pumps allow fluid entry from both sides, providing enhanced hydraulic balance and higher efficiency, ideal for larger flow rates and higher pressure applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial and utility needs.</p></p>
