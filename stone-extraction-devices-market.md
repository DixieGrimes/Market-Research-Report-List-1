<p><p>The stone extraction devices market primarily comprises two types: stone extraction balloons and stone extraction baskets. Stone extraction balloons are flexible devices used to capture and remove stones from the urinary tract or bile ducts, utilizing inflation to facilitate extraction. In contrast, stone extraction baskets are wire mesh tools designed to entrap and retrieve stones during endoscopic procedures. Both devices are essential in minimally invasive surgeries, enhancing the efficiency and safety of stone removal in various medical practices.</p></p>
