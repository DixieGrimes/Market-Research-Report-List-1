<p><ul><li>Constant Energy Magnets</li><li>Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetotherapy equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Constant Energy Magnets and Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field (PEMF) Energy. Constant Energy Magnets utilize static magnetic fields for therapeutic benefits, promoting pain relief and improved circulation. In contrast, PEMF Energy involves the use of alternating magnetic fields that can penetrate deeper into tissues, enhancing cell function and regeneration. Both types aim to alleviate pain and improve overall well-being but operate through different mechanisms and applications tailored to varied healthcare needs.</p></p>
