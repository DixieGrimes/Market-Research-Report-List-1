<p><strong>The Wafer Level Underfills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No-Flow Underfill (NUF)</li><li>Non-Conductive Film (NCF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Level Underfills (WLUF) market comprises primarily two types: No-Flow Underfill (NUF) and Non-Conductive Film (NCF). NUF is a liquid epoxy material applied between the die and substrate, providing robust adhesion and protection during reflow processes without requiring additional flow. In contrast, NCF is a solid film that adheres upon heat and pressure application, ensuring uniform coverage. Both types enhance reliability and thermal performance in semiconductor packaging, essential for advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
