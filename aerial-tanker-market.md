<p><ul><li>Probe-and-drogue Type</li><li>Flying Boom Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerial tanker market comprises primarily three types: probe-and-drogue, flying boom, and other systems. The probe-and-drogue method features a flexible hose with a drogue at the end, allowing aircraft equipped with a probe to connect easily during refueling. In contrast, the flying boom system uses a rigid arm to deliver fuel, typically suited for larger military aircraft. The "other" category may include innovative or hybrid systems, reflecting advancements in technology and varied military or commercial needs.</p></p>
