<p><p>The coated paper market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and woodfree. Mechanical coated paper is produced using wood pulp that retains natural fibers, offering a lower cost and good print quality, primarily utilized for magazines and brochures. In contrast, woodfree coated paper is made from chemically treated wood pulp, resulting in a smoother finish and superior brightness. This high-quality paper is ideal for premium products like art books and glossy packaging, catering to a more discerning audience.</p></p>
