<p><p>The hard coal market primarily consists of two types: Lump Anthracite and Anthracite Fines. Lump Anthracite refers to larger, solid chunks of coal valued for their high carbon content and low impurities, making them suitable for heating and industrial applications. Conversely, Anthracite Fines are smaller particles that result from the mining and processing of anthracite, typically used in applications such as water filtration and as a fuel alternative. Both types play crucial roles in energy production and industrial processing.</p></p>
