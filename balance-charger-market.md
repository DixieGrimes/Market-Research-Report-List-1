<p><p>The balance charger market is divided into two main types: AC balance chargers and DC balance chargers. AC balance chargers are designed to connect directly to mains electricity, offering high charging efficiency and supporting multiple battery types simultaneously. In contrast, DC balance chargers operate on low voltage, typically used in portable or remote settings, making them ideal for hobbyists and users with off-grid applications. Both types prioritize battery health by ensuring even charging across individual cells, enhancing performance and longevity.</p></p>
