<p><p>Glass hydrophobic coatings are advanced surface treatments designed to repel water, dirt, and other contaminants from glass surfaces. This technology is widely applicable in various sectors, including automotive, construction, electronics, and consumer goods, enhancing the durability and cleanliness of glass surfaces.</p><p>The market for glass hydrophobic coatings is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by increasing demand for improved visibility in automotive applications, as well as in architectural and industrial settings.
