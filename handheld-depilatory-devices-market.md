<p><p>Handheld depilatory devices utilize two primary technologies: Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) and laser. IPL devices emit broad-spectrum light, targeting hair pigmentation to destroy follicles, suitable for various skin tones. They offer wider coverage and quicker treatments. In contrast, laser devices focus on specific wavelengths, providing precision in targeting hair, making them more effective for certain skin and hair types. Both types aim for long-term hair reduction, appealing to consumers seeking convenient, at-home hair removal solutions.</p></p>
