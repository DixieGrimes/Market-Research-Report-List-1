<p><ul><li>Air Cooled Scroll Chiller</li><li>Air Cooled Screw Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air-cooled chiller market includes two primary types: air-cooled scroll chillers and air-cooled screw chillers. Air-cooled scroll chillers utilize a scroll compressor, providing efficient cooling for residential and light commercial applications, ideal for smaller loads. In contrast, air-cooled screw chillers employ screw compressors, designed for larger commercial and industrial settings, offering greater cooling capacities and energy efficiency. Both types are crucial for temperature control, yet they cater to different scales of cooling requirements and operational demands.</p></p>
