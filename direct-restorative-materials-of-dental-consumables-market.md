<p><p>Direct restorative materials in the dental consumables market include various types used for tooth restoration procedures. Amalgam consists of a mixture of metals, providing durability and strength for posterior teeth. Composite materials offer aesthetic appeal and can match tooth color, making them suitable for visible areas. Glass ionomers release fluoride and bond well to dentin, making them ideal for certain applications. Other materials may include resin ionomers or ceramics, each serving distinct purposes in restorative dentistry, catering to patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
