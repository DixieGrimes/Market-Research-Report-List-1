<p><p>The electric vehicle brake caliper market features two primary types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers are simpler and lighter, providing adequate braking force for smaller or less powerful electric vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers offer enhanced braking performance and reliability, distributing force more evenly across the brake pad, making them suitable for high-performance electric vehicles. As electric vehicle demand grows, the choice between these caliper types increasingly reflects the vehicle's performance requirements and design considerations.</p></p>
