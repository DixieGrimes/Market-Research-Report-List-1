<p><strong>The GPS Module with Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active GPS Attenna Module</li><li>Passive GPS Attenna Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS module market features two main types of antennas: active and passive. Active GPS antenna modules contain built-in amplifiers that enhance signal strength, making them ideal for applications requiring high sensitivity and performance, such as automotive and handheld devices. In contrast, passive GPS antenna modules lack internal amplification and rely on the strength of incoming signals, typically used in less demanding applications where size and cost are priorities, such as basic tracking systems. Both serve distinct market needs.</p></p>
