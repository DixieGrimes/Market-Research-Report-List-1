<p><p>The sheet metal equipment market includes various types of machinery essential for metal fabrication. Power presses utilize mechanical force for stamping and shaping materials. Arbor presses offer manual operation for precision tasks. Swaging machines shape metal through axial compression, while bending machines create angles in sheets using mechanical or hydraulic force. Hydraulic shearing machines are designed to cut sheet metal at specified thicknesses, offering efficiency and accuracy in production processes. Each type fulfills unique roles in metalworking applications.</p></p>
