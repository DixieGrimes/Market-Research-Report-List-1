<p><p>The flexi-bag market is categorized into monolayer and multilayer types based on the layering of materials. Monolayer flexi-bags consist of a single layer, providing adequate protection and cost-effectiveness for various applications like bulk storage and transportation. Multilayer flexi-bags, on the other hand, are made of multiple layers, enhancing barrier properties against moisture, chemicals, and oxygen, making them suitable for sensitive products and longer storage durations. Both types cater to different industry needs, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
