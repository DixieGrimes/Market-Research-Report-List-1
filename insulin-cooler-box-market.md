<p><p>The insulin cooler box market comprises two main types: plug-in and without charge options. Plug-in cooler boxes utilize an electrical power source to maintain the optimal temperature for insulin storage, ensuring consistent cooling. In contrast, without charge cooler boxes rely on passive cooling technologies, such as gel packs or thermal insulation, making them portable and ideal for situations where electricity is unavailable. Both types cater to the needs of diabetes patients by preserving insulin efficacy during travel or daily activities.</p></p>
