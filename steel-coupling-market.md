<p><p>The steel coupling market encompasses various types, primarily rigid and flexible couplings. Rigid couplings are used to connect shafts that are perfectly aligned, providing strong torque transmission without accommodating misalignment. In contrast, flexible couplings allow for some degree of misalignment and can absorb vibrations, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and energy, ensuring efficient power transmission and minimizing wear in machinery systems.</p></p>
