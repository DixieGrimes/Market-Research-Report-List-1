<p><p>The Europium market primarily consists of two oxidation states: Eu(II) and Eu(III). Eu(II) is mainly used in the production of phosphors for LED lighting and televisions, offering high brightness and improved energy efficiency. In contrast, Eu(III) is widely employed in glass, ceramics, and as a dopant in various applications for its strong luminescent properties. The differentiation in applications drives demand, with Eu(II) favoring modern display technologies and Eu(III) focusing on traditional industrial uses.</p></p>
