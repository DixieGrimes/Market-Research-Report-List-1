<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotating position sensor market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors operate by physically touching the rotating element, providing reliable position feedback but may wear out over time. In contrast, non-contact sensors detect position without physical contact, using technologies like magnetic or optical sensing, which enhances durability and reduces maintenance needs. Each type caters to specific applications, with contact sensors often found in industrial equipment and non-contact sensors commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
