<p><strong>The Coolant Switch Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Way</li><li>3 Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant switch valve market is categorized primarily into two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. </p><p>2-way valves facilitate a simple flow control mechanism, directing coolant either in or out of a system, primarily used in straightforward applications. Conversely, 3-way valves offer more versatility by enabling the mixing or diverting of coolant flows from multiple sources or to various destinations, often employed in more complex systems to enhance efficiency and temperature regulation. Both types are vital in HVAC and automotive cooling systems.</p></p>
