<p><p>The disposable medical gloves market comprises several key types: latex gloves, nitrile gloves, PVC gloves, and others. Latex gloves offer excellent dexterity and comfort but may cause allergic reactions. Nitrile gloves are latex-free, providing chemical resistance and a robust barrier, making them popular in healthcare settings. PVC gloves are a cost-effective option, suitable for low-risk tasks, but lack the elasticity of nitrile and latex. Other glove types may include neoprene and vinyl, catering to specific user needs in various medical environments.</p></p>
