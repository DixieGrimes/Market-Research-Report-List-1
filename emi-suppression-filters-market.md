Lead type filters feature leads for direct mounting, making them suitable for various applications requiring stability and reliability. Block type filters are larger, designed for high-power applications, and provide robust noise suppression. Each type addresses specific needs in electronic devices to enhance performance and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
