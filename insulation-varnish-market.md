<p><ul><li>Impregnating Varnish</li><li>Finishing Varnish</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulation varnish market comprises primarily two types: impregnating varnish and finishing varnish. Impregnating varnish is designed to penetrate and saturate winding materials in electrical equipment, enhancing insulation and moisture resistance. It protects against environmental factors and improves electrical performance. Finishing varnish, on the other hand, is applied as a top layer for added protection and aesthetic appeal. It provides an additional barrier against dust, chemicals, and abrasion, ensuring longevity and operational efficiency of electrical components.</p></p>
