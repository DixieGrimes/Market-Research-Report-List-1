<p><p>The LiDAR PCB (Printed Circuit Board) market encompasses two primary types: Airborne LiDAR and Terrestrial LiDAR. Airborne LiDAR employs aircraft or drones to capture detailed topographic data over large areas, making it ideal for mapping and environmental monitoring. In contrast, Terrestrial LiDAR is ground-based, utilizing stationary or mobile systems to generate high-resolution 3D models of smaller, localized environments, such as buildings or infrastructure. Both technologies play vital roles in applications like urban planning, forestry, and disaster management.</p></p>
