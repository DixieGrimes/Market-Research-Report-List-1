Three Flow Breathing Exercise Devices feature three distinct pathways for inhalation and exhalation, promoting multifaceted respiratory training. Two Flow Breathing Exercise Devices allow for dual pathways, enhancing both inhalation and exhalation resistance for balanced lung work. Single Flow Breathing Exercise Devices focus on one airflow channel, offering a straightforward approach to improve lung capacity and respiratory strength. Each type targets specific therapeutic or training needs for users.</p></p>
