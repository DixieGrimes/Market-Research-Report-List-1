STR Analysis focuses on identifying specific repeating sequences in DNA, which helps verify cell line authenticity and detect contamination. SNP Analysis targets variations in single nucleotide sequences, providing insights into genetic differences and lineage. Other methodologies may include gene expression profiling and sequencing techniques, enhancing overall characterization for research, therapeutic, and biopharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
