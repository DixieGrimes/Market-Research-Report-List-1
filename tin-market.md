<p><p>The tin market is classified into three main types: the pyrogenic process, the electrolytic process, and others. The pyrogenic process involves extracting tin from ores through high-temperature smelting, resulting in high-purity metal. The electrolytic process uses electrochemical methods to refine tin, providing a more environmentally friendly alternative with high efficiency. Other methods include refining scrap tin or using specific chemical methods for niche applications. Each process varies in efficiency, cost, and environmental impact, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
