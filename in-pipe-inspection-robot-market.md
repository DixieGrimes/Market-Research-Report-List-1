<p><p>The in-pipe inspection robot market comprises two primary types: wheel in-pipe inspection robots and tracked in-pipe inspection robots. Wheel in-pipe inspection robots utilize wheels for mobility within pipelines, offering maneuverability on smooth surfaces. They are typically suited for urban settings with well-maintained infrastructure. In contrast, tracked in-pipe inspection robots employ rubberized tracks, enabling better grip and stability on uneven or rough surfaces.
