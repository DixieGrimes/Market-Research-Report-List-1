<p><p>The ophthalmic laser devices market comprises various types, each serving distinct therapeutic purposes. Diode lasers are commonly used for retinal and refractive surgery, while femtosecond lasers enable precise corneal cuts and cataract procedures. Excimer lasers are vital for refractive surgeries like LASIK. Nd:YAG lasers treat conditions like glaucoma and cataracts, while argon lasers are used for retinal phototherapy. Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT) lasers provide a non-invasive option for glaucoma treatment, emphasizing the diverse applications of these advanced devices in ophthalmology.</p></p>
