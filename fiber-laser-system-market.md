<p><p>The fiber laser system market can be categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Crystal Lasers** utilize solid-state crystals for high power and efficiency, ideal for cutting and welding applications. </p><p>2. **Nonlinear Lasers** harness nonlinear optical processes, enabling frequency conversion for specific wavelengths. </p><p>3. **Rare Earth Doped Lasers** incorporate rare earth elements like ytterbium or neodymium, providing enhanced performance and efficiency. </p><p>4. **Plastic Lasers** employ plastic optical fiber, offering flexibility and lightweight options for diverse applications, including communication and sensing. Each type serves unique industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
