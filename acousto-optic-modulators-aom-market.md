<p><p>Acousto-optic modulators (AOM) are devices that utilize the interaction between light and sound waves to modulate laser beams in terms of intensity, frequency, or direction. AOMs are widely used in applications such as laser scanning, telecommunications, imaging systems, and in various scientific research fields due to their high speed and efficiency.</p><p>The AOM market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced laser technologies across multiple industries, including healthcare, defense, and telecommunications.
