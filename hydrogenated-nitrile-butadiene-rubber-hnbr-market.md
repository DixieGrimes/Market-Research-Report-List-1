<p><p>Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (HNBR) is a versatile elastomer known for its excellent resistance to chemicals, heat, and wear. It is categorized into three market types based on acrylonitrile (ACN) content: Low ACN HNBR, which offers good mechanical properties and lower oil resistance; Medium ACN HNBR, balancing performance between oil and heat resistance; and High ACN HNBR, which provides superior oil resistance but may compromise flexibility. Each type serves diverse applications in automotive, industrial, and oilfield sectors.</p></p>
