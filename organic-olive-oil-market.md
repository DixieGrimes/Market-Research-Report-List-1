<p><p>The organic olive oil market comprises three main types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Olive Oil, and Olive Pomace Oil. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is the highest quality, cold-pressed without chemicals, retaining rich flavors and nutrients. Olive Oil is a blend of refined and virgin oils, offering good quality at a lower price. Olive Pomace Oil is derived from the leftover pulp of olives after extraction, often refined and lower in quality, suitable for general cooking.</p></p>
