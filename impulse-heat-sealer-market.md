<p><p>The impulse heat sealer market includes two main types: foot impulse heat sealers and hand impulse heat sealers. Foot impulse heat sealers are operated using a foot pedal, allowing for hands-free sealing, ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, hand impulse heat sealers require manual operation, offering portability and versatility for smaller tasks. Both types utilize heat to create airtight seals on plastic materials, making them essential in packaging industries, food preservation, and retail, catering to varying user needs and production scales.</p></p>
