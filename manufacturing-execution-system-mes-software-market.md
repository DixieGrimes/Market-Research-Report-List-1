<p><p>Manufacturing Execution System (MES) software can be categorized into two main types: Point MES and Integrated MES. Point MES solutions focus on specific manufacturing processes or functions, offering targeted capabilities for tasks such as production tracking, quality management, or maintenance scheduling. In contrast, Integrated MES systems provide a comprehensive approach, uniting various manufacturing processes and data across the entire production lifecycle, ensuring seamless communication and real-time visibility among all manufacturing functions, leading to improved efficiency and decision-making.</p></p>
