<p><p>The Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment market is primarily categorized into two types based on pump systems: Single Roller Pump and Double Roller Pump equipment. Single Roller Pump systems utilize a single roller mechanism to circulate blood, offering simplicity and reduced complexity. In contrast, Double Roller Pump systems feature two rollers, enhancing flow control and reliability, especially in complex surgeries. Both types are crucial for maintaining blood circulation and oxygenation during cardiac procedures, with distinct advantages suited to varying surgical requirements.</p></p>
