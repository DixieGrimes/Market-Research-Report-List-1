<p><p>The neuroblastoma treatment drugs market comprises various medications used in managing this pediatric cancer. Cyclophosphamide is an alkylating agent that disrupts DNA synthesis. Dinutuximab and Naxitamab are monoclonal antibodies that target GD2, a tumor-associated antigen. Doxorubicin hydrochloride is an anthracycline antibiotic that interferes with DNA replication. Vincristine sulfate is a vinca alkaloid that inhibits cancer cell division. The "Other" category includes additional therapies and supportive care drugs utilized in treatment regimens. Together, these drugs enhance treatment efficacy and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
