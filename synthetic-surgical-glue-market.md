Polymeric hydrogel offers flexible, biocompatible options that hydrate tissues and are ideal for applications requiring moisture retention. Urethane-based adhesives provide enhanced mechanical strength and adhesion to various tissues, making them suitable for challenging conditions. Together, these types cater to diverse surgical needs, enhancing wound closure and healing processes.</p></p>
