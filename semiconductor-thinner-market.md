<p><strong>The Semiconductor Thinner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Removal</li><li>Negative Photoresist Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor thinner market is divided into two main types: positive and negative photoresist removal. Positive photoresist removal involves using solvents to eliminate the unexposed areas of photoresist films, allowing for precise patterning in microfabrication. Conversely, negative photoresist removal targets exposed regions, where the remaining unexposed content is washed away, resulting in desired patterns. Both methods are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring high-quality lithography for integrated circuits and enhancing overall production efficiency.</p></p>
