<p><p>Metabolism testing includes various analyses that assess an individual's metabolic health. VO2 Max analysis measures aerobic capacity and endurance levels by determining the maximum oxygen consumption during exercise. Body composition analysis examines the percentage of fat, muscle, and other tissues, providing insights into overall fitness and health. Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) analysis estimates the number of calories burned at rest, which helps tailor nutrition and exercise plans. Together, these tests inform personalized health and fitness strategies, driving market growth.</p></p>
