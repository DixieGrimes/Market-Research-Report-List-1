<p><p>The veterinary trocars market is segmented into three key types: orthopedic, thoracic, and laparoscopic. Orthopedic trocars are used in joint surgeries, facilitating precise access to bones and tissues. Thoracic trocars are designed for procedures within the chest cavity, aiding in the management of respiratory issues. Laparoscopic trocars are essential for minimally invasive surgeries, allowing for less trauma and quicker recovery. Each type addresses specific surgical needs in veterinary medicine, enhancing overall patient outcomes and surgical efficiency.</p></p>
