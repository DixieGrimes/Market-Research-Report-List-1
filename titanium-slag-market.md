<p><ul><li>Acid Soluble Slag</li><li>Chlorination Slag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Slag Market includes Acid Soluble Slag and Chlorination Slag, both derived from ilmenite processing. Acid Soluble Slag is used primarily in sulfate-process TiO2 pigment production, offering high titanium content and low impurities. Chlorination Slag, with higher titanium dioxide purity, is ideal for chloride-process TiO2 pigment and titanium metal production. Both types cater to industries like paints, plastics, and aerospace, with demand driven by their efficiency and cost-effectiveness in downstream applications.</p></p>
