<p><ul><li>Single-punch</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium speed tablet presses are essential in the pharmaceutical and food industries for tablet production. They are primarily categorized into two types: single-punch and rotary tablet presses. Single-punch presses operate with a single station, compacting powder into tablets one at a time, making them suitable for small-scale production. In contrast, rotary tablet presses employ multiple tooling stations on a rotating turret, allowing for higher output and efficiency, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Both types balance speed and precision in tablet formulation.</p></p>
