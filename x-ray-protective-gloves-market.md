<p><p>The X-Ray Protective Gloves market features two primary types: lead gloves and lead-free gloves. Lead gloves are designed with lead materials to offer superior radiation protection, often preferred in high-exposure environments. However, they can be heavier and less flexible. Lead-free gloves, on the other hand, utilize alternative radiation-shielding materials that reduce weight and improve dexterity, making them appealing for prolonged use. Both types serve to safeguard medical personnel and patients during diagnostic and therapeutic imaging procedures.</p></p>
