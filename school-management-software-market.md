<p><p>School management software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access, automatic updates, and scalability while reducing hardware costs. It allows for greater collaboration and accessibility for users from various locations. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on school servers, offering greater control over data and security. However, it typically requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to diverse institutional needs.</p></p>
