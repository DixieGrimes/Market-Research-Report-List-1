<p><p>Crude Oil Flow Improvers (COFIs) encompass a range of additives designed to enhance the flow characteristics of crude oil. Paraffin inhibitors prevent the formation and deposition of waxes, while asphaltene inhibitors mitigate the aggregation of heavy hydrocarbons. Scale inhibitors prevent mineral deposits in pipelines and equipment. Drag reducing agents streamline flow by reducing turbulence, and hydrate inhibitors prevent the formation of ice-like structures in subsea pipelines. Together, these categories optimize crude oil transport and improve efficiency in the oil industry.</p></p>
