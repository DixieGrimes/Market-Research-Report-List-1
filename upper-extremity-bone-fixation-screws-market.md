<p><p>The upper extremity bone fixation screws market comprises three main types: stainless steel, titanium, and bioabsorbable screws. Stainless steel screws offer strength and durability, making them a common choice for rigid fixation. Titanium screws are favored for their lightweight properties and biocompatibility, reducing the risk of allergic reactions. Bioabsorbable screws are designed to dissolve over time, eliminating the need for removal surgeries and promoting natural healing. Each type serves specific patient needs, influencing their selection in orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
