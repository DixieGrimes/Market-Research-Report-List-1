<p><p>The GNSS module market encompasses various types based on capabilities. Multi-Constellation modules utilize signals from multiple satellite systems, enhancing accuracy and availability. Multi-Frequency modules operate on varied frequency bands, improving precision and reducing interference. Augmentation modules provide additional corrections for enhanced accuracy in specific applications. Differential GNSS modules utilize spatially separated receivers to enhance location precision through real-time corrections. Other types may include specific applications or integrations with other technologies, catering to diverse market needs and use cases.</p></p>
