<p><p>Military anti-jamming antenna systems are crucial for maintaining reliable communication in contested environments. Nulling systems work by actively cancelling out jamming signals through interference, enhancing the clarity of legitimate communications. Beam steering systems, on the other hand, dynamically direct the antenna's focus towards desired signals while ignoring jamming sources, ensuring optimal signal strength and quality. Both types play a vital role in securing military operations and enabling effective command and control under adverse conditions.</p></p>
