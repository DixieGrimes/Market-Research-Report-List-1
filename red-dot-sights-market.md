<p><p>The red dot sights market includes three main types: full-size, microdots, and mini reflex sights. Full-size red dots provide a larger viewing window, ideal for tactical uses and primarily mounted on rifles, promoting quick target acquisition. Microdots are compact, lightweight options designed for pistols or as secondary optics, offering versatility without added bulk. Mini reflex sights strike a balance, being small enough for handguns yet providing ample visibility, making them suitable for a variety of firearms applications, from competitive shooting to self-defense.</p></p>
