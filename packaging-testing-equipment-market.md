<p><p>The packaging testing equipment market encompasses various types designed to evaluate the integrity and durability of packaging materials. Tensile strength testing equipment measures the force required to pull materials apart, assessing their durability. Compression testing equipment evaluates a package's ability to withstand external forces without deformation. Drop testing equipment simulates falls to determine how well packaging protects its contents. Together, these tools ensure that packaging can endure handling and transportation, safeguarding products from damage.</p></p>
