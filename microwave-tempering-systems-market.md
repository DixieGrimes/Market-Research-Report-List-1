<p><strong>The Microwave Tempering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tunnel Microwave Tempering Systems</li><li>Box Microwave Tempering Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microwave tempering systems are crucial for food processing, particularly in thawing and tempering frozen products. Tunnel microwave tempering systems facilitate continuous processing by allowing products to move through a heated tunnel, ensuring uniform heating and efficiency. In contrast, box microwave tempering systems are designed for batch processing, where products are placed inside a chamber for targeted heating. Both systems enhance productivity and product quality, catering to different operational needs in the food industry.</p></p>
