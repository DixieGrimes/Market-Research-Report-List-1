<p><ul><li>Single Gas</li><li>Binary Mixture</li><li>Ternary Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding gas market is categorized into three main types: single gas, binary mixture, and ternary mixture. Single gas involves using one type of gas, such as argon or helium, for specific welding applications. Binary mixtures combine two gases, like argon and carbon dioxide, to enhance welding quality and arc stability. Ternary mixtures consist of three gases, optimizing performance for complex tasks by balancing characteristics such as heat input, penetration, and material compatibility for various welding processes.</p></p>
