<p><p>The griddle spatula market can be categorized into two main types: slice type and with seam. The slice type spatula features a flat, wide blade ideal for flipping and serving food, providing smooth motion for delicate items like pancakes or fish. In contrast, spatulas with seam are designed with a raised edge or a channel, which helps in capturing and lifting food more effectively, making them suitable for porous or crumbly items like burgers or grilled vegetables. Both types cater to specific cooking needs.</p></p>
