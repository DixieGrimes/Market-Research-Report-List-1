<p><strong>The Aromatherapy Diffusers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Nebulizer</li><li>Evaporative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aromatherapy diffusers enhance the therapeutic benefits of essential oils, categorized mainly into three types: ultrasonic, nebulizer, and evaporative. Ultrasonic diffusers use water and ultrasonic waves to create a fine mist, dispersing oils effectively while maintaining their properties. Nebulizer diffusers atomize oils without water, delivering a potent aroma and maximum therapeutic benefits. Evaporative diffusers use fan-assisted airflow to evaporate oils, allowing for a lighter scent experience. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different preferences in aroma diffusion.</p></p>
