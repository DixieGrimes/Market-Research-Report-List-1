<p><p>The Air Cargo Pallet Market is categorized into two main types: Main Deck Pallets and Lower Deck Pallets. Main Deck Pallets are designed for larger aircraft, capable of carrying heavier and bulkier cargo. They are often used in freighters and larger passenger planes. Conversely, Lower Deck Pallets are utilized in the aircraft's lower storage compartments, accommodating smaller and lighter cargo. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing space and ensuring safe transport of goods in the aviation industry.</p></p>
