<p><strong>The Temperature Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog-to-digital converters (ADCs)</li><li>Digital-to-analog converters (DACs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature converter market comprises two primary types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs transform analog temperature signals from sensors into digital data for processing and analysis, making them essential in modern electronic devices. Conversely, DACs convert digital data back into analog signals, often used in applications that require outputting temperature readings to display devices or actuators. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and industrial automation, ensuring precise temperature measurements and controls.</p></p>
