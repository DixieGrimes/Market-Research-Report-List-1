<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Pssive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Diesel Particulate Filter (SiC DPF) market is categorized into Active and Passive systems. Active systems use external energy, such as fuel burners or electric heaters, to regenerate the filter by burning off accumulated soot, ensuring efficient performance in low-temperature conditions. Passive systems rely on exhaust heat and catalytic coatings to naturally oxidize soot, making them cost-effective and low-maintenance. Both types aim to reduce emissions, with active systems offering better control and passive systems providing simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
