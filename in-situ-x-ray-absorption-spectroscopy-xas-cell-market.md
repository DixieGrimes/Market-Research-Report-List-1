<p><p>In-situ X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (XAS) cells are essential for studying materials under real-world conditions. The Transmission XAS (TM-XAS) market focuses on measuring the absorption of X-rays passing through a sample, providing insights into electronic and geometric structures. Conversely, the Fluorescent XAS (FL-XAS) market emphasizes the detection of emitted X-ray fluorescence from the sample, offering information about local environments and oxidation states. Both methods are critical for characterizing complex materials in various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
