<p><p>The offset ink market can be categorized into several types, primarily focusing on flexo water-based and flexo solvent-based inks, along with other variants. Flexo water-based inks are environmentally friendly and ideal for non-porous substrates, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. Flexo solvent-based inks, on the other hand, provide excellent adhesion and durability but may have higher VOC levels. Other inks encompass various formulations, including UV-curable and rubber-based options, catering to diverse printing applications and substrate compatibility.</p></p>
