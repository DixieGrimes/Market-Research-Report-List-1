<p><p>The elastomer-coated fabrics market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Silicone coated fabrics offer high-temperature resistance and flexibility, ideal for automotive and industrial uses. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) coated fabrics provide excellent abrasion and chemical resistance, making them suitable for outdoor gear and medical applications. TPO (thermoplastic olefin) coated fabrics combine durability and lightweight characteristics for automotive interiors. Rubber coated fabrics are known for their enhanced grip and waterproof qualities, widely used in protective gear and industrial applications.</p></p>
