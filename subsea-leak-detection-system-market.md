<p><p>The Subsea Leak Detection System market comprises two primary types: Active and Passive systems. Active Subsea Leak Detection Systems utilize sensors and monitoring technology to actively detect leaks by continuously gathering data and send alerts when anomalies are identified. In contrast, Passive Subsea Leak Detection Systems rely on naturally occurring signals, such as pressure changes or chemical signatures, to identify leaks without active monitoring. Both systems aim to enhance underwater safety and environmental protection in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
