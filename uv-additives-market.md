<p><strong>The UV Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Stabilizers</li><li>Light Stabilizer</li><li>UV Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV additives market consists of three primary types: heat stabilizers, light stabilizers, and UV absorbers. Heat stabilizers enhance the thermal stability of materials, preventing degradation when exposed to high temperatures. Light stabilizers protect materials from damage caused by UV radiation, prolonging their lifespan. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV rays, converting them into less damaging energy, thereby safeguarding the integrity of plastics and coatings. Together, these additives are essential for improving the durability and performance of products in various applications.</p></p>
