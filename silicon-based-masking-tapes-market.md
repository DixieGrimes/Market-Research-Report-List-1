<p><p>Silicon-based masking tapes are essential in various industries for protection and surface finishing. The market is segmented into two main types: single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided tapes feature adhesive on one side, making them ideal for surface protection and light-duty applications. In contrast, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding capabilities for more demanding tasks, such as mounting and fastening. Both types offer high temperature resistance and clean removal, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
