<p><ul><li>Passive Power Dividers</li><li>Active Power Dividers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Dividers market is categorized into passive and active power dividers. Passive power dividers distribute power without requiring additional energy, often found in applications like communication systems and RF circuitry. They are favored for their reliability and low cost. Active power dividers, on the other hand, amplify the signal before distribution, ensuring greater signal integrity across longer distances. These are often utilized in advanced telecommunications and broadcasting systems, providing enhanced performance in scenarios requiring high precision and control.</p></p>
