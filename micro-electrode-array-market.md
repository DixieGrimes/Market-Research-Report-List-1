<p><ul><li>Classical MEA</li><li>Multiwell-MEA</li><li>CMOS-MEA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microelectrode array (MEA) market comprises three main types: Classical MEA, Multiwell-MEA, and CMOS-MEA. Classical MEAs feature fixed electrode configurations for electrophysiological measurements, providing high spatial resolution. Multiwell-MEAs incorporate multiple wells to allow parallel testing of multiple samples, enhancing throughput for research and drug screening. CMOS-MEAs utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, enabling integrated signal processing and lower power consumption. Collectively, these MEA types cater to diverse applications in neuroscience, stem cell research, and drug development.</p></p>
