<p><p>The electronic fuse IC market features two main types: the auto retry type and the latched type. The auto retry type resets automatically after a fault, allowing for continuous operation without manual intervention. In contrast, the latched type requires a manual reset after a fault condition, offering enhanced protection by preventing further damage until the issue is resolved. These distinctions cater to different application needs, influencing their adoption in sectors like automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
