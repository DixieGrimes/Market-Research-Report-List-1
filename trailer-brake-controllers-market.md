<p><p>The trailer brake controllers market features two primary types: proportional brake controllers and time-delayed brake controllers. Proportional brake controllers provide real-time braking response by adjusting the trailer brakes based on the tow vehicle's braking force, ensuring smoother and more synchronized stopping. In contrast, time-delayed brake controllers engage brakes after a preset delay, applying power at a fixed rate regardless of the tow vehicleâ€™s braking behavior. Each type caters to different towing needs, enhancing safety and control while hauling trailers.</p></p>
