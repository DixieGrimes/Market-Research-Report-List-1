LPCVD (low-pressure CVD) is favored for producing high-quality silicon films in a controlled environment. MOCVD (metal-organic CVD) is utilized for depositing compound semiconductors, often used in advanced solar technologies. Other types may include variations tailored for specific materials or processes in solar cell production.</p></p>
