<p><p>Autoclaved Lightweight Concrete (ALC) Panels are innovative building materials characterized by their lightweight, high insulation properties, and fire resistance. Made from a mixture of sand, cement, lime, and water, ALC panels are cured in an autoclave under high temperature and pressure, resulting in a durable product with excellent thermal and acoustic insulation capabilities. These panels are increasingly used in various construction applications, including residential, commercial, and infrastructural projects, owing to their energy efficiency and environmentally friendly characteristics.</p><p>The market for ALC panels is witnessing significant growth driven by the increasing demand for sustainable construction materials and rapid urbanization across emerging economies.
