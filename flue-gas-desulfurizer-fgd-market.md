<p><p>Flue Gas Desulfurizers (FGD) are technologies used to remove sulfur dioxide (SO2) from exhaust flue gases in power plants and industrial processes. The market segments include: </p><p>1. **Dry FGD**: Utilizes dry sorbents to capture SO2, offering lower water consumption but requiring high sorbent use.</p><p>2. **Semi-Dry FGD**: Combines elements of both wet and dry systems, using a slurry that absorbs SO2 while producing less wastewater.</p><p>3. **Wet FGD**: Involves a liquid solution, typically limestone or lime, to efficiently scrub SO2 from flue gases, generating a byproduct for disposal.</p></p>
